When used with Intalio|Designer you have the option of preview and editing the Intalio|AJAX form with the browser of your choice.  By double-clicking the form it will be opened up for editing inside designer.  To use another browser, you can right click the form and select *Edit with...* or *Preview with...* and choose the browser of your choice.
